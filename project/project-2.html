SwipeJobb App Design

SwipeJobb is the ultimate AI-driven job search app. It offers a seamless, intelligent way to connect users with their ideal job opportunities.

Role: Product Designer

Team: 2 Designers, 3 Developers

Location: Malmo, Sweden

Duration: Malmo, Sweden

Mission

SwipeJobb leverages cutting-edge AI technology to match candidates with job openings, ensuring an ideal fit for both parties. This advanced approach enhances the accuracy of job placements, creating a smoother experience for candidates and employers alike.

Additionally, the platform streamlines the recruitment process, making it quick and hassle-free from start to finish. By offering seamless, intelligent hiring solutions, we are transforming the way employers connect with top talent, making hiring more efficient and effective than ever before.

Essential Steps

Engaged in rapid prototyping to quickly align the product with market demands, ensuring it consistently meets user expectations and effectively addresses real-world problems. This approach enables agile adjustments that keep the product relevant and responsive to evolving needs.

Conducted multiple in-depth interviews with a diverse range of users to deeply understand their pain points and specific needs. These insights shed light on the key areas that could enhance user satisfaction and usability.

Key Outcomes

Daily active users (DAU) rose by 20% following the implementation of a personalized recommendations feature, enhancing engagement by offering content tailored to user preferences.

User error rates decreased by 30% thanks to the addition of intuitive input validation and contextual hints, making navigation easier and reducing user frustration.

App load time improved by 40% through refining design assets and optimizing the UI/UX architecture, providing a faster, smoother experience that keeps users engaged and satisfied.
